MaterialsCommons FAQ

How do I sign up for an account?
Both Materials Commons Projects and Materials Commons Data have registration pages. You only need to register once. An account on one site creates an account on both.
Can I install my own version of MaterialsCommons?
The process for installing your own version of MaterialsCommons is not currently documented. MaterialsCommons has a lot of dependencies. Our roadmap includes releasing a containerized version of the MaterialsCommons. This will allow you to easily get your own version of the site up and running using Docker.
How do I know my data is safe?
The MaterialsCommons backend uses an Isilon mirrored storage cluster. We keep redundant copies of your data to ensure its safety and availability.
What happens to my data if MaterialsCommons goes away?
While we are working hard to make sure that MaterialsCommons has a bright and sustainable future, in the event that MaterialsCommons does go away all your data will continue to be available. We are teaming with other groups (TBA) to ensure that data stored in MaterialsCommons will always be available.
How do I learn more?
Head on over to our COMMUNITY page. We are always interested in working with other members of the community.
